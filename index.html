Project WIN

From WIN to EFA

Project WIN (Wealth Insured Navigation) laid the groundwork for accessible financial literacy at HCC. Its momentum, team culture, and student impact became the launchpad for the Economic & Finance Association.

The spark

WIN started as a student-driven initiative to demystify personal finance for peers.

Building blocks

Guides, workshops, and resource packs created trust and repeat engagement across campus.

The launch

That community and momentum became the foundation for EFA’s broader mission in markets and leadership.

Who we are

Where ambition meets discipline, the Economic & Finance Association (EFA) forges visionaries. We analyze, innovate, and lead, empowering students to turn financial insight into real-world impact and setting the standard for leadership.

What we do

The Economic & Finance Association (EFA) is more than a student organization, it’s a movement of forward-thinking leaders driven by purpose, discipline, and innovation. At EFA, ambition meets execution. Our members are future economists, investors, policymakers, and entrepreneurs who don’t just react to change, they create it.

We equip our members with analytical tools, strategic mindset, and professional confidence to navigate and shape today’s dynamic financial landscape. Through research, collaboration, and hands-on experience, EFA transforms bold ideas into measurable results. Preparing students to lead in boardrooms, markets, and policy arenas alike.

EFA believes that leadership begins with understanding of markets and people, along with purpose. We challenge our members to think critically, plan strategically, and act decisively. We are not spectators of progress; we are architects building pathways that define the future of finance and economics at Houston City College and beyond.
